Why Track Your Expenses?

Tracking your expenses allows you to:

  • Understand where your money is going
  • Identify areas where you can save money
  • Plan and budget effectively
  • Achieve your financial goals

Why Save Money?

Saving money is important because it:

  • Provides financial security
  • Allows you to achieve your long-term goals
  • Provides a safety net for unexpected expenses
  • Opens up opportunities for investments and wealth building

Why Set Financial Targets?

Setting financial targets helps you:

  • Stay focused on your financial goals
  • Motivate yourself to save and invest
  • Track your progress and make adjustments as needed
  • Plan for major expenses like buying a house or retiring
